02. FAQ

Freequently Asked
Questions

At CareMax Services, we understand that choosing the right care provider is an important decision. Below are some common questions to help you understand our services and how we can support you or your loved ones.

Is there support for me at home?

Yes! CareMax Services provides personalized in-home care tailored to your needs, including personal assistance, household support, and transport services to help you stay independent in your home.

I am worried about a loved one. How can Oberlin help?

We offer compassionate, professional support for individuals needing assistance with daily tasks, medical care, and social engagement. Our goal is to enhance independence, dignity, and quality of life for every client.

  • Personalized care plans tailored to individual needs
  • A focus on emotional well-being and community participation

How can I manage my mobility challenges?

Our team specializes in mobility support, including assistance with walking, transfers, and the use of mobility aids. We also provide exercise and therapy sessions to improve strength and independence.

What services do you offer for pain management?

We provide holistic pain management solutions, including medication assistance, therapy referrals, and personalized wellness programs to ensure comfort and relief.
